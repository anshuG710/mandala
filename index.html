About Chiya Mandala

Welcome to Chiya Mandala, where tradition meets taste in the bustling neighborhood of Tinkune, Kathmandu. Our tea shop is more than just a place to grab a cup of tea – it's a cultural experience that celebrates the rich heritage of Nepali tea culture.

Founded with a passion for authentic flavors and community connection, we source our teas directly from the pristine hills of Nepal, ensuring every cup tells a story of tradition, quality, and love.

Step into our cozy space and discover why locals consider us their home away from home.
